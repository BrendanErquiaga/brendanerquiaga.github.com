Who are we?

We are two Game Designers who currently live in lovely Phoenix, AZ. We make games in our spare time as a hobby, a passion and hopefully as a career.

We are part of a group called Real Dedicated. Real Dedicated is a fellowship of technology professionals and alumni who share a strong passion for making games. Real Dedicated means you willingly sacrifice time and sleep to complete a project. Real Dedicated means everything you create is an attempt to achieve a professional level of quality and to better yourself.

So. Aside from making games together, we went to college together. This came with it's fair share of drinking games, as you can imagine. Over the years we've played them all, we've even made a few. Eventually we decided to put our brains together and make Beer Pong better. And that lead you here!

Also we like beer.

Why? No Seriously, Why?

Because it's fun. Because we could. And because we were inspired by a friend.

We wanted to take the inspiration of merging Role Playing and Beer Pong to a new level. We wanted to flex our design muscles and put things like class diversity and strategic planning into a game about throwing balls into cups.

Our background as game designers forced us to think critically about which classes to include, why to include them and how the hell you play support in Beer Pong. We aren't done tweaking the game but we have made leaps and bounds from the original version.

One thing we've really enjoyed, this is a pure design challenge. There's no GUI, there's no code; just a game and trying to have fun while understanding the rules.
