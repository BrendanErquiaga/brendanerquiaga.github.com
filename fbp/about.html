Who are we?

We are two Game Designers who currently live in lovely Phoenix, AZ. We make games in our spare time as a hobby, a passion and hopefully as a career.

So. Aside from making games together, we went to college together. This came with it's fair share of drinking games, as you can imagine. Over the years we've played them all, we've even made a few. Eventually we decided to put our brains together and make Beer Pong better. And that lead you here!

Also we like beer.

But...why?

Because it's fun. Because we could. And because we were inspired by a friend.

We wanted to take the inspiration of merging Role Playing and Beer Pong to a new level. We wanted to flex our design muscles and put things like class diversity and strategic planning into a game about throwing balls into cups.

Our background as game designers forced us to think critically about which classes to include, why to include them and how the hell you play support in Beer Pong.
