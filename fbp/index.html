Fantasy Beer Pong - Beta

Fantasy Beer Pong is essentially an alternative rule set to beer pong that is intended to add a level of skill, tactics and fun into the already successful game. In Fantasy Beer Pong every player takes the role of a class, and through this class they drastically change the way they play the game. You have been invited to play the beta, so read up and get ready.

Familiar Classes

The classes in Fantasy Beer Pong are derived from classical fantasy archtypes. Each class comes with it's own unique abilities and play style. Will you play an Assassin, sneaking shots in for extra cups? Or perhaps a Guardian, protecting your cups with Shield Block?
