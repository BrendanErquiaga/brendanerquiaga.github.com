Fantasy Beer Pong

Fantasy Beer Pong is an alternative rule set to beer pong that is intended to add a level of skill, tactics and fun into the already successful game. In Fantasy Beer Pong every player takes the role of a class, and through this class they drastically change the way they play the game. You have been invited to play the beta, so read up and get ready.
