Description

In early 2010 myself and several teams consisting of 2-9 members created 3 games for the Microsoft Surface. This was a unique design challenge and our first foray into game projects. Because I had taken 1 programming class, I decided to take the role of programmer.

Of the 3 projects, Kateas was my personal favorite. This simple color-match game will be ported to mobile platforms soon
