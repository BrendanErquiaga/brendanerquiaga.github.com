trade secret

Trade Secret is a 2.5D action platformer set in a post-Apocalyptic underground civilization. Trade Secret features a unique Z-axis transition mechanic that allows players to explore levels that are beautifully rendered in 3D, while keeping the gameplay in 2D. Another innovative feature is the game's tinkering system that allows players to craft custom armor and weapons.

magic missile

Magic Missile is a first-person puzzle game in which players solve both physics and scripted puzzle events with a ball of magic fired from their hand. It is made in Unity 3D.

Players assume the role of a nameless mage who has been locked away in a magical tower. The player's only tool is a ball of magic that can be summoned and thrown from his hand.

Merchant UI Programmer PC Suspended

Merchant is a tycoon game made in the Unity 3D engine; I was responsible for creating the GUI.

Merchant is a game based inside a fantasy RPG. Instead of controlling the hero of the story, you control the merchant that the hero buys and sells goods from. You will be responsible for helping the heroes by selling them things they need, while buying all of the items they do not want. The heroes are persistent characters in game, so they will return to you if you keep them happy.
